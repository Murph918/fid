VW Bugs

There were millions and millions of VW bugs made and shipped to America. Unfortunately you see less and less of them on our streets. The are these easiest cars to work on and still get parts for. Why not join us and find one and bring it back to life? Old Bugs are so easy to work on, you can literally take the engine out by yourself as they are only kept in by 4 bolts! These cars are so small you can keep them in your garage and take them completely apart with space to spare.
